How to Choose Your Ideal Charger

When selecting your Anker portable charger, consider these three factors:

  1. Device Compatibility: Do you need to charge a laptop (Prime), an iPhone wirelessly (MagGo), or a standard USB-C phone (Nano)?
  2. Capacity vs. Weight: High capacity (Prime) means more weight. Low weight (Nano) means fewer total charges.
  3. Charging Speed: If you are always on the move, look for high-wattage outputs (20W+) to minimize the time your phone is attached to the battery.
